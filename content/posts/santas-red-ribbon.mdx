Red Ribbon winner of the American Homebrewers Association Winterbrew Competition. The original extract reciepe came from The Joy of Homebrewing by Charles Papazian.

## Ingredients

### Fermentables

* 8.7 lb Belgian Pilsner
* 0.5 lb UK Crystal 60L
* 0.2 lb American Black Patent
* 0.2 lb Belgian Biscuit
* 0.2 lb Belgian Aromatic
* 0.75 lb honey (added to boil)

### Hops

* 1 oz Cascade (60 min)
* 2 tbsp ginger root (8 min)
* 1 oz cinnamon stick (8 min)
* 0.5 oz Saaz (2 min)

### Yeast

* Wyeast Belgian Abbey Ale II - 1762

## Brew Date

* September 2, 2015
* Original Gravity: 1.056
* Final Gravity: 1.005
