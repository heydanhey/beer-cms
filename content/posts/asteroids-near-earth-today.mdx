Did you ever wonder how many asteroids are flying by Earth today?

**Me, too!**

This app was built to test out [Remix's](https://remix.run/) cool loader function and how easily the app can **deployed to edge**.
Uses the [NASA open API](https://api.nasa.gov/) to track incoming asteroids.
