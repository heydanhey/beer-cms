The comeback kid. A nice an easy batch to evaluate my equipment and memory.

## Ingredients

### Fermentables

- 12.5 lb 2-Row Breiss
- 1.4 lb Caramel 40L

### Hops

- 1.25 oz Centennial,Yakima Chief 10.1% (60 min)
- 1.25 oz Cascade, Yakima Chief 5.8% (20 min)
- 1 oz Cascade, Yakima Chief 5.8% (1 min)

### Yeast

- Wyeast America Ale II

## Brew Date

- February 5, 2022
- Original Gravity: 1.060

## Keg Date

- February 20, 2022
- Final Gravity: 1.010
