Simple stout.

## Ingredients

### Fermentables

- 10 lb Maris Otter 
- 1.25 lb Flaked Barley
- 12.5 oz Chocolate Malt
- 12.5 oz Roasted Barley

### Hops

- 2.5 oz Fuggle Pellets,Yakima Chief 4.7% (60 min)
- 1.25 oz Fuggle Pellets,Yakima Chief 4.7% (20 min)

### Yeast

- Wyeast London Ale

## Brew Date

- February 27, 2022
- Original Gravity: 1.055

- Kegged on April 2, 2022
- Final Gravity: 1.012
