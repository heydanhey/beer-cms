Injuries play a large role in the NBA. I follow the Chicago Bulls and I like to stay up to date on the [injury status](https://injuries.heydanhey.com/) of they players. I use this app to scrape the latest injury report and updates from a popular sports website. The real fun part was getting to work with begin.com. Wow, what a cool service! It's built on top of Architect which helps provision all AWS resources for you.
