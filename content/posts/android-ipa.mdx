## Ingredients

### Grains

* 10 lb 2-row Malteurop 
* 0.75 50L Cargill
* 0.75 White Wheat Cargill

### Hops

* 1 oz Galena (60 min)
* 1 oz Centennial (30 min)
* 1 oz Hallertau (1 min)
* 1 oz Centennial (Dry)

### Yeast

* Wyeast American Ale II

## Brew Date

* September 25, 2015
* Original Gravity: 1.060
* Final Gravity: 1.015
